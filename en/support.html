FAQ

Do I need the internet?

No. Search, sentence parsing and audio work offline. The network is only used for external links and for anonymous usage statistics.

Where do the translations come from?

English meanings come from JMdict, with other open sources for names, kanji, furigana and pitch accent. The full list with licences is on the home page and on the “About” screen in the app.

How often is the dictionary updated?

The content is updated periodically — we aim for an update roughly twice a year, pulling in fresh data from the source projects. Found an error in an entry? Report it to the source (JMdict) and the fix arrives with an update.

How does privacy work?

Your queries, opened words and history never leave the device. Details are in the privacy policy.

Can I add my own dictionary?

Yes. The app imports dictionaries in the od-dict/1 format — see the format description.
