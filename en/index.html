Ordinary Japanese Dictionary is built for everyday reading and study. Detailed entries with example sentences, proper names, kanji breakdowns, furigana and pitch accent — and all of it works offline. Inspired by jisho.org.

Features 機能

Everything works without the internet: the dictionary, morphological analysis and audio all live inside the app.

圏

Offline

Search and reading need no network. Nothing loads on the fly.

辞

Rich dictionary

Full English meanings with examples, tags and cross-references.

例

Example sentences

Real Japanese examples from Tatoeba, linked to each sense.

読

Reading mode

Paste a sentence — the app splits it into words and shows readings and meanings.

仮

Furigana & pitch

Readings above the kanji and a pitch-accent contour.

探

Type anything

Kanji, kana or romaji. Understands inflections: わからない → 分かる.

筆

Handwriting & camera

Draw a kanji you don't know, or recognise text from a photo (OCR).

声

Voice & audio

Voice search and native-speaker pronunciation.

漢

Kanji breakdown

On'yomi and kun'yomi readings, components, JLPT and WaniKani levels.

＋

Your own dictionaries

Add several dictionaries and import your own — import format →

史

History & export

Your lookup history stays on the device; export it to CSV.

言

Three UI languages

English, Russian and Japanese — follows your system or your choice.

How it looks 画面

Screenshots from a device.

Private by default 秘

Your query text, opened words and history never leave the device and are not sent to analytics. The app shows no ads and does not use precise location. More in the privacy policy.
