Privacy Policy

Ordinary Japanese Dictionary · Google Play · effective 26 June 2026 · other versions

This app works offline. The app doesn't collect your queries, opened words or history. There is no sign-up and no ads, and precise location is not used. The data sent over the network is anonymous and not linked to you (details below).

What we don't collect

  • your query text, opened words and translations;
  • your history (it stays on the device);
  • your name, email or account;
  • precise (GPS) location.

What is sent

Anonymous usage events — the action, but not its content (for example: the script of a query, its length, the number of results — never the text itself). Together with standard technical data (app and OS version, device model, country derived from IP) and an anonymous per-service identifier that is not linked to your identity. Crash and stability data are collected by the Google Play platform.

Services

Statistics are processed by Mixpanel and PostHog (USA), each under its own privacy policy.

Permissions

Microphone — only for voice input; camera — only for text recognition from a photo (OCR); files — when importing a dictionary or exporting history; network — for statistics and external links. Voice input uses the built-in Android recogniser; external links open in your browser only when you tap them.

Contact

You can clear your history and remove the app along with all local data. Questions: notononoto@gmail.com. Changes to this policy are published on this page.
