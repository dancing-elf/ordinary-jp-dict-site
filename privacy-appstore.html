Privacy Policy

Ordinary Japanese Dictionary · App Store · effective 5 July 2026 · other versions

This app can work offline. The app doesn't collect your queries, opened words or history. There is no sign-up and no ads; the advertising identifier (IDFA) and tracking (ATT) are not used.

What is sent

Usage statistics and crash reports: device model, OS and app version, device and installation identifiers, approximate location derived from the IP address, and usage events — never the text of your queries. There are no accounts, so this data doesn't include your name or email.

Services

Processed in the USA, each under its own privacy policy: Amplitude and PostHog — statistics, Sentry — crashes, AppsFlyer — install attribution (without IDFA or ATT).

Permissions

Microphone — only for voice input; camera — only for text recognition from a photo (OCR); files — when importing a dictionary or exporting history; network — for statistics and external links. Voice input uses the built-in iOS recogniser (it may use the network when on-device recognition isn't available); external links open in your browser only when you tap them.

Contact

You can clear your history and remove the app along with all local data. Questions: notononoto@gmail.com. Changes to this policy are published on this page.
