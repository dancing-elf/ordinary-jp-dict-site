Ordinary Ancient Greek Dictionary is for reading classical texts and study. Type any inflected form — the app identifies it, finds the lemma and shows the entries, with declension and conjugation tables nearby. All offline.

Features

Everything works without the internet: the dictionaries, the morphological analysis and the text recognition live inside the app.

Any inflected form

ἦν, εἶπεν, λόγῳ — the dictionary parses the form and finds the lemma.

Three dictionaries

LSJ and Middle Liddell in English, plus Dvoretsky's Greek–Russian.

Reading mode

Paste a passage — the app splits it into words and shows the translations.

Inflection tables

Declension and conjugation right on the word card.

Any input

With or without diacritics, a transliteration (logos → λόγος), or a word from the translation.

Camera (OCR)

Recognize Greek text from a photo.

Examples

The Gospel of John with a parallel English (ASV) or Russian translation.

Your dictionaries

Interface language

English, Russian — and, if you like, Ancient Greek.
